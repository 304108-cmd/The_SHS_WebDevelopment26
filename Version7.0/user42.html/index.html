Short Story

Inside a quiet village near the mountains, a curious teenager named Mason dreamed of becoming an inventor. Every afternoon, he worked inside his small garage building strange machines out of old metal and broken electronics. His neighbors often laughed at his unusual ideas, but Mason never gave up on his experiments.

One stormy evening, Mason discovered a glowing object buried beneath a pile of tools in the corner of the garage. The object was smooth, silver, and covered in mysterious symbols that shimmered in the dark. As soon as he touched it, the garage lights flickered wildly and the windows rattled from the powerful energy.

Mason slowly stepped through the doorway and entered a massive underground laboratory filled with futuristic technology. Strange robots moved silently across the room while giant computer screens displayed maps of distant planets. From that moment forward, Mason knew his ordinary life had changed forever.
